# Reforge acquisition readiness

Whether already maps to Reforge’s operator-first ethos: macro signals → decisions → shareable
leadership guidance. This document captures the narrative, artifacts, and readiness checklist to
present Whether as a Reforge-grade product strategy OS.

## Positioning narrative (Reforge-ready)
1. **Macro signals → leadership clarity**: translate the yield curve into plain-English operating
   constraints with explicit source provenance.
2. **Decision guidance → repeatable workflows**: Decision Shield outputs become reusable
   templates for hiring, roadmap, pricing, and capital posture.
3. **Historical precedent → confident execution**: Time Machine and Insight Database show how
   similar regimes historically mapped to product and engineering outcomes.
4. **Auditability → enterprise lock-in**: Decision Memory + assumption locking prevents
   hindsight rewriting and creates a defensible decision trail.

## Flagship artifacts (what leaders share)
- **Executive briefing pack**: one-click export for Slack/email/slide-ready briefs with citations.
- **Leadership decision brief**: copy-ready summary that fits into quarterly planning decks.
- **Role-specific posture board**: CFO/COO/CTO/CPO presets that map constraints to their workflows.
- **Regime playback**: Time Machine narrative showing regime shifts and operational deltas.

## Acquisition readiness checklist
- [ ] **Executive brief CTA** is primary in the Overview header.
- [ ] **Weekly/monthly briefing APIs** are stable and output copy-ready content with citations.
- [ ] **Insight Database** includes precedent case studies tied to regimes.
- [ ] **Decision Memory** logs are immutable, exportable, and attached to sources.
- [ ] **Role-based presets** are shipped for CFO/COO/CTO/CPO.
- [ ] **Source health panel** surfaces stale or failed data feeds.
- [ ] **Live macro fetchers** (CPI, unemployment, BBB spreads) replace snapshot-only inputs.

## Demo flow (Reforge-style)
1. Open Overview → show live regime + constraints + provenance.
2. Trigger a Decision Shield scenario → export the verdict copy.
3. Jump to Time Machine → replay a past regime shift.
4. Export executive brief → show “ready for leadership sync” artifact.

## Success signals to validate
- Weekly brief adoption by leadership teams.
- Decision Shield outputs shared in planning docs.
- Repeat usage of Time Machine for strategy retrospectives.
- Decision Memory entries referenced during post-mortems.
